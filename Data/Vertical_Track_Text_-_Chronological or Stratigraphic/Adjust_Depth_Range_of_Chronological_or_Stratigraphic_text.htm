Adjust Depth Range of Chronological or Stratigraphic text

Using the Mouse

  1. Hover the cursor over the vertical text entry border you wish to adjust.

  2. Hold the right mouse button down, drag the border to the desired depth and release the mouse button.

 

With Precision

  1. Right click on the text you wish to edit, and select Track Text properties. The Track Text Entry will open. 

  2. Edit the Depth Start and End values as required.

  3. Click Apply then Close.
